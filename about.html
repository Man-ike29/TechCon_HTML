History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. What started as a modest meetup of 50 developers and entrepreneurs has evolved into one of the world's premier technology conferences, attracting over 10,000 attendees annually from across the globe.

Over the years, TechCon has been at the forefront of technological innovation, hosting groundbreaking announcements and fostering connections that have shaped the tech industry. From the early days of mobile computing to the current era of artificial intelligence and quantum computing, TechCon has consistently provided a platform for visionaries to share their ideas and inspire the next generation of technologists.

Mission

Our mission is to accelerate technological innovation by bringing together the brightest minds in the industry. We believe that breakthrough technologies emerge from the intersection of diverse perspectives, collaborative thinking, and bold experimentation.

TechCon serves as a catalyst for meaningful connections, knowledge sharing, and the advancement of technologies that will shape our future. We are committed to fostering an inclusive environment where ideas can flourish, partnerships can form, and the next generation of tech leaders can emerge.

Through our carefully curated sessions, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities, we aim to:

  • Showcase cutting-edge technologies and emerging trends
  • Facilitate meaningful connections between industry professionals
  • Provide educational opportunities for skill development
  • Support diversity and inclusion in the tech community
  • Inspire innovation and entrepreneurship

Past Speakers

TechCon has been honored to host some of the most influential voices in technology. Our speakers have included industry pioneers, innovative entrepreneurs, and thought leaders who have shaped the digital landscape.

Dr. Sarah Chen

AI Pioneer & Former Google Executive

Dr. Chen revolutionized machine learning algorithms during her tenure at Google and now leads breakthrough research in ethical AI. Her TechCon 2023 keynote on "The Future of Human-AI Collaboration" was attended by over 5,000 participants and sparked industry-wide discussions on responsible AI development.

Marcus Rodriguez

Cybersecurity Expert & SecureNet Founder

As the founder of SecureNet, Marcus has protected millions of users from cyber threats. His presentation on "Zero-Trust Architecture in the Cloud Era" at TechCon 2022 provided actionable insights that many companies have since implemented to strengthen their security posture.

Dr. Aisha Patel

Quantum Computing Researcher & IBM Fellow

Dr. Patel's groundbreaking work in quantum computing has earned her recognition as one of the leading researchers in the field. Her TechCon 2021 session on "Quantum Advantage: From Theory to Practice" demystified quantum computing for thousands of attendees and inspired a new wave of quantum startups.

James Kim

Blockchain Innovator & Ethereum Core Contributor

James has been instrumental in developing key protocols that power the Ethereum ecosystem. His TechCon 2020 workshop on "Building Decentralized Applications" provided hands-on experience that launched numerous successful DeFi projects and established him as a sought-after mentor in the blockchain space.
